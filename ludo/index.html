How to Play

Welcome to the Sliding Puzzle game! The goal is to arrange the numbered tiles in ascending order, from left to right and top to bottom.

Click on a tile that is adjacent (up, down, left, or right) to the empty space to slide it into the empty spot.

Keep track of your moves and time, and try to solve the puzzle as fast as you can!
